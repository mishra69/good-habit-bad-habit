About This Habit Counter

Welcome to your personal habit balancer! Blue notes represent positive habits you've completed, while red notes track habits you're trying to break. Drag notes to the balance area to record your progress. Notes of opposite colors will cancel each other out, showing your true habit balance visually. Watch your net score increase as good habits outweigh the bad ones.
